# Claude Mythos — What It Changes for Gustia
Date: 2026-04-08

---

## Bottom Line

Gustia doesn't need Mythos. The use case is narrow enough that GPT-4o mini handles it. But Mythos changes the competitive landscape — more tools, more platforms, more AI-native competitors will emerge.

---

## What Changes for Gustia

### What Gets Better
- **AI conversation quality** — Mythos handles edge cases, unusual questions, multi-turn reasoning significantly better
- **Context window** — hypothetical capability to handle very long menu conversations
- **Multilingual** — Mythos is better at PT, FR, ES, IT nuances in restaurant context
- **Error recovery** — Mythos self-corrects mid-conversation more reliably

### What Doesn't Change
- **Core product** — QR scan → chat → order. Doesn't require frontier AI.
- **Latency** — Mythos is likely slower than GPT-4o mini
- **Cost** — Not publicly available, likely 10-20x more expensive per call
- **Integration complexity** — OpenAI API is already working

---

## Competitive Landscape

| Competitor type | Impact of Mythos |
|----------------|----------------|
| Other restaurant AI concierge apps | Will get better. More features, better conversation. |
| Big tech (Google, Meta) | AI waiters coming. More competition. |
| Custom-built solutions | Cheaper to build. More entrants. |
| Gustia's moat | NOT the AI quality. The product, the UX, the sales, the relationships. |

## Honest Score

| Dimension | Score | Why |

## What to Watch

- If Anthropic releases Opus-level equivalent publicly → evaluate migration
- If OpenAI releases GPT-5 → likely comparable or better at lower cost
- Gustia's real moat is distribution (QR placement), not AI intelligence

---

## When to Revisit

- First 10 paying customers acquired
- Competitors starting to match feature parity
- AI costs dropping significantly
