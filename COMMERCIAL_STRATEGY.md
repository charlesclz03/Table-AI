# Table IA - Commercial Strategy
**Date:** 2026-04-03
**Status:** Locked operating model for the current cash-first phase

---

## Core Offer

Table IA is not being sold as cheap self-serve SaaS.

It is being sold as a **founder-installed restaurant concierge**:

- **EUR 299 setup**
- **First month included**
- **EUR 49/month after that**

---

## What the EUR 299 setup includes

- Menu and knowledge-base setup
- Restaurant-specific concierge personality and rules
- QR code generation
- Launch configuration
- First correction round after launch

This must be framed as installation and launch work, not as an arbitrary access fee.

---

## Positioning

Use:

- "AI concierge for restaurants"
- "multilingual table assistant"
- "customers can ask the menu anything"
- "I set it up for your restaurant"

Avoid:

- "AI waiter"
- "replaces staff"
- "generic chatbot"
- "cheap SaaS"

---

## Why this model is correct right now

- It creates upfront cash fast enough to matter.
- It matches the real service effort involved in onboarding.
- It still leaves room for recurring revenue.
- It fits Portugal better than premium US pricing while avoiding the trap of EUR 29/month volume-chasing.

---

## Sales rule

The goal of the next meetings is **not** to collect interest.

The goal is to close:

- paid setup
- live launch
- recurring billing starting in month 2

If the demo does not make EUR 299 feel reasonable, the pitch or the target restaurant needs to change.

---

## ICP for now

Best targets:

- tourist-heavy restaurants
- multilingual environments
- places with frequent menu questions
- owners or managers physically present
- restaurants that already tolerate QR usage

Avoid for now:

- highly traditional low-tech places with no QR acceptance
- restaurants demanding POS integration immediately
- restaurants where allergen liability dominates every conversation

---

## Offer discipline

Do not expand the promise during sales.

Table IA v1 is:

- menu Q&A
- recommendations
- wine pairing guidance
- hours / practical FAQ
- multilingual assistance

Table IA v1 is not:

- allergy authority
- order automation
- POS integration
- reservation management
- full waiter replacement
