# Table IA — Name Council
**Date:** 2026-04-02
**Status:** ✅ CLOSED — Winner: **Table IA**

## Decision

**Chosen:** Table IA
**Reason:** French/PT style for "Table AI", clear, honest, doesn't conflict with Angel AI (D2C)

---

## The Problem

Current name: **"Table IA"**

Issues:
- "Angel AI" conflicts with the D2C Angel AI project
- "B2B" sounds corporate, not warm
- Not memorable
- Restaurant owners won't feel proud to say "I use Table IA"

---

## What We Need

A name that:
1. ✅ Feels warm and professional for restaurants
2. ✅ Available as domain (.com, .pt, or .ai)
3. ✅ Doesn't conflict with Angel AI (D2C)
4. ✅ Easy to say, easy to remember
5. ✅ Reflects: AI + restaurant table + conversation

---

## Candidate Names

### Tier 1 — Strong

| Name | Why It's Good | Risk |
|------|--------------|------|
| **Mesa** | Portuguese for "table" — the TABLE is where the AI lives. Simple, memorable, local feel. mesa.ai available? | Could feel too local (Portuguese only) |
| **TableAI** | Direct. The AI on your table..com likely available | Generic, no warmth |
| **ConciergeAI** | Exactly what it does. Professional. | Common — hard to get .com |
| **Serve** | Warm, action-oriented. "We serve." Available? | Could mean anything |

### Tier 2 — Interesting

| Name | Why It's Good | Risk |
|------|--------------|------|
| **Foyer** | Hotel lobby — first impression, welcome. Warm. | Slightly formal |
| **Gust** | From "restaurant gusto." Energetic. | gust.ai likely taken |
| **Motto** | Memorable, works in any language | No obvious connection |
| **Ora** | Latin for "to pray" — prayer = conversation. Short, beautiful | Hard to understand |
| **Ordine** | Italian for "order" — restaurant order, orderly | Italian, not Portuguese |
| **Atende** | Portuguese for "attends" — the AI that attends to guests | Portuguese only |

### Tier 3 — Wildcards

| Name | Why It's Good | Risk |
|------|--------------|------|
| **TalkMenu** | Exactly what it does | Generic |
| **AskMesa** | Combines "ask" + Portuguese "mesa" | Awkward |
| **QrConcierge** | Describes exactly what it is | Boring |
| **Tabled** | "Tabled for now" — but also a table | Confusing |

---

## Names Ruled Out

❌ **Angel AI** — conflicts with D2C project
❌ **Table IA** — corporate, conflicts
❌ **Restaurant AI** — too generic
❌ **Menu AI** — too narrow

---

## The Question

**What feeling should the name evoke?**

- [ ] **Warmth** — like a friendly waiter (Mesa, Concierge)
- [ ] **Intelligence** — clever, technical (TableAI, Ora)
- [ ] **Hospitality** — hotel-grade service (Foyer, Serve)
- [ ] **Simplicity** — just works (TalkMenu, AskMesa)

**Mesa** is my top pick:
- Portuguese word — local, authentic
- The table is where the customer meets the AI
- mesa.ai — probably available
- Easy to say in any language (mee-sa)
- Warm, not technical
- Doesn't conflict with any existing project

Second: **ConciergeAI** — professional, clear
