# Table IA — Restaurant Outreach Scripts
**Date:** 2026-04-02

---

## Pre-Visit Research (Before Walking In)

Before visiting any restaurant:

1. Google Maps → reviews, photos, hours, rating
2. TheFork → menu, prices, ratings
3. Instagram → vibe, photos, engagement
4. TripAdvisor → what reviewers complain about

**What to look for:**
- Rating 4.0+ (means they care about customer experience)
- Tourist-heavy or mixed crowd (AI helps language barrier)
- QR codes already on tables (they're QR-friendly)
- Staff answering questions (lots of FAQ = AI opportunity)

## In-Person Pitch (2 minutes)

### Opening
```
"Hi, I noticed you're [restaurant name] — I was just walking by and saw the reviews mentioning [specific dish or thing]. I actually wanted to ask — do you find customers ask a lot of questions about the menu?"
```

### Qualify
```
"How do you handle questions like 'what wine goes with the lamb?' or 'is there a vegetarian option?' — do staff answer those?"
```

### Demo
```
"I'm building something that might help with that. It's an AI concierge — customers scan a QR code on the table and can ask the AI anything about the menu in any language.

Can I show you a quick demo on my phone?"
[OPEN DEMO — ask 3 questions:
1. "What's your signature dish?"
2. "What wine pairs with the lamb?"
3. "Do you have vegetarian options?"]
```

### Close
```
"What do you think — could you see this being useful at your tables?"
```

### If Interested
```
"Perfect. It's €299 for full setup — includes menu setup, QR codes, your first month live. After that, €49/month.

Want me to send you the details?"
```

### If Hesitant
```
"No worries. Would it help if I left you a QR code to try at one table for a week? No commitment."
```

## What NOT to Say

❌ "I'm selling AI" — too abstract
❌ "It uses GPT-4" — they don't care
❌ "It'll replace your staff" — threatening
❌ "It's free" — sounds suspicious
❌ "Sign up online" — kills the relationship

✅ "Your customers can ask the menu anything"
✅ "Like a smart waiter that knows everything"
✅ "I set it up for you with your menu and QR codes"
